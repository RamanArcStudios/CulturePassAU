### Usage Guidelines

**Primary Teal** is your workhorse â€” use it for all primary actions, active nav states, and interactive elements. It reads as modern and trustworthy without feeling corporate.

**Cosmic Purple** pairs with teal in gradients and works well for premium/featured labels, category badges, and anything that needs to feel exclusive or special.

**Ember Orange** should be used sparingly â€” it's your attention-grabbing accent for sold-out warnings, limited availability, and notification dots. Overusing it kills its impact.

**Dark mode** should feel like a night out â€” deep blacks with a slight blue-navy tint (`#0D0F14`) rather than pure black, giving depth without harshness. Surfaces layer subtly on top.

**Light mode** stays clean and airy â€” off-whites and soft greys keep the focus on event imagery and content.
